VIKRAM-S

Mission “Prarambh” meaning “the beginning” in Sanskrit, involves the launch of our Vikram-S vehicle, which is a single-stage solid-fuelled sub-orbital vehicle for testing various sub systems and technologies that would be used in our Vikram orbital class vehicles – Vikram-I, Vikram-II and Vikram-III. It will be carrying three customer payloads.

Vikram-S is INDIA's first private launch vehicle besides being WORLD's first high-acceleration(14g) full-fledged composite vehicle. Step by step, we achieved one development milestone after another. Despite pandemic related delays, our team remained agile and focused. We have developed new cutting-edge technologies, including carbon composites and 3D printing within the Indian industry.

This is a great milestone for all of us at Skyroot, and a pathbreaking one as the pioneering launch mission of the Indian private sector. Our feeling is nothing short of exuberance.
